Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?

MiroFish doesn't predict with certainty — it reveals plausible futures. By simulating hundreds of diverse agents, it surfaces trends, risks, and reactions that traditional analysis misses. Think of it as a stress test for your strategy.

What kind of data do I need to upload?

Any text document: news articles, policy drafts, product specs, financial reports, even novel chapters. PDF, TXT, and Markdown files are supported. The more context you provide, the richer the simulation.

Is my data secure?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each user's projects are completely isolated. We never use your data to train AI models.

Can I use my own LLM?

Yes. MiroFish supports any OpenAI-compatible API, including GPT-4, Claude, Qwen, and self-hosted models. Bring your own API key.

How many agents can I simulate?

Depends on your plan. Starter supports up to 20, Pro supports 50, and Enterprise is unlimited. Each agent has a unique personality, memory, and behavioral profile.

What happens after my trial ends?

Your projects and reports are preserved. You can still view and export them, but creating new simulations requires an active subscription.
