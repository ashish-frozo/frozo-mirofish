Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions? expand_more

Augur doesn't predict with certainty — it reveals plausible futures. By simulating diverse autonomous agents, it surfaces trends, risks, and reactions that traditional analysis misses. Think of it as a stress test for your strategy.

What kind of data do I need to upload? expand_more

Any text document: news articles, policy drafts, product specs, financial reports, even novel chapters. PDF, TXT, and Markdown files are supported. The more context you provide, the richer the simulation.

Is my data secure? expand_more

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS. Each user's projects are completely isolated. We never use your data to train AI models.

Can I use my own LLM? expand_more

The self-hosted version supports any OpenAI-compatible API, including GPT-4, Claude, Qwen, and self-hosted models. The hosted platform uses a pre-configured model.

How many agents can I simulate? expand_more

Depends on your plan. Starter supports up to 20, Pro supports 50, and Enterprise is unlimited. Each agent has a unique personality, memory, and behavioral profile.

What happens after my trial ends? expand_more

Your projects and reports are preserved. You can still view and export them, but creating new simulations requires an active subscription.
